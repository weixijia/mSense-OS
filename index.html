Capture · Process · Export

A pipeline you can drop into your lab today.

01 · Capture

Synchronized ingest

Every sensor thread timestamps against one host clock, with an optional microcontroller for hardware-level sync.

  • Per-frame timestamps
  • Multi-radar, no interference
  • Live preview on launch
02 · Process

Real-time on-device

3D FFT to Range-Doppler and Range-Azimuth on the GPU; pose overlays computed in parallel on its own thread.

  • CUDA / MPS / CPU auto-select
  • Viridis heatmaps, live
  • Off-UI-thread by design
03 · Export

Research-ready output

Structured, timestamped artifacts per session — ready for training pipelines and analysis.

  • Video MP4 · Audio WAV
  • mmWave HDF5 + metadata
  • Skeleton JSON, per person
Temporal alignment

When everything shares a clock, fusion becomes trivial.

mSense OS anchors capture to a single master clock and logs the offset of every stream, so downstream models see a coherent multimodal timeline instead of four drifting recordings.
